## Evaluation Instructions

Produce a thorough assessment across 5 dimensions (each 0-100):

1. **technical_fit** (weight: 25%): How well do the candidate's demonstrated skills and knowledge match the technical requirements? Consider both CV evidence and interview responses.

2. **communication_quality** (weight: 20%): How clearly and effectively does the candidate communicate? Consider structure of answers, articulation, and ability to convey complex ideas.

3. **cultural_fit** (weight: 20%): Based on the candidate's values, work style, and responses, how well would they integrate into a professional MENA workplace? Consider collaboration signals, professionalism, and adaptability.

4. **growth_potential** (weight: 20%): Does the candidate show learning agility, ambition, and capacity for growth? Consider career trajectory, self-awareness, and curiosity demonstrated in responses.

5. **experience_depth** (weight: 15%): How deep and relevant is the candidate's practical experience? Consider specificity of examples, problem-solving approaches, and real-world impact.

## Scoring Rubric
- 85-100: Exceptional — top-tier candidate, strongly recommended
- 70-84: Strong — above average, recommended for advancement
- 55-69: Moderate — meets baseline, some gaps to address
- 40-54: Below Average — significant concerns
- 0-39: Poor fit — major misalignment with role requirements

## Evidence Requirements
For EVERY claim you make, cite specific evidence:
- From CV: quote relevant sections
- From video transcripts: quote specific answers
- From AI scores: reference specific per-question performance
